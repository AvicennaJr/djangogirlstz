Who we are

Django Girls Tanzania is a non-profit organization and community that empowers women by organizing free, one-day programming workshops. We provide participants with mentorship, resources, and a supportive environment to learn the basics of web development using Django and Python.

We are a volunteer-run organization with hundreds of people contributing to bring more amazing women into the world of technology.
